4word (2024)
4word is a strategic multiplayer vocab and building game. I, along with two other teammates fully developed this game, including cards, a box, building pieces, as well as the rules and a rule sheet. 4word cards provide a word description and challenge a player to match it to the correct word out of the given four choices. Depending on the card’s difficulty, the player is then given a select amount of building pieces to add to the structure, being careful to keep it balanced. The player to use all of their pieces first wins. In this project I contributed to the overall ideation, logo design, and box design. Designs were made in Adobe Illustrator.

On the Move! (2024)
On the Move! is an information design piece details my walking statistics from February 16 to February 20, 2024. Throughout this period I personally tracked the paths I took and locations I went to. The map is designed to reflect my most frequent activities and personal landmarks, such as Boston MBTA stations, my work place, and the places I go to study. A bar graph at the bottom displays the ranges of distances traveled throughout the 5 days.

Exquisite Corpse Online! (2024)
Exquisite Corpse Online! is a website developed over the course of 48 hours during the hackathon, HackBeanpot 2024. This project was created using HTML, CSS, and Javascript through Visual Studio Code, with the help of 4 other teammates. I was tasked with creating the majority of the user interface. The website is based off the classic drawing game, “exquisite corpse”, in which 3 players draw the head, body, and legs of a creature respectively, without seeing what the other drew. In the end the images are put together to create a silly amalgamation.
